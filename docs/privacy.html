Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 24, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how RentDue (project name: RentCollect) handles information when you use the app.

2. Information Processed in the App

  • Property records (name, group, notes, units)
  • Lease records (tenant names, rent amount, dates)
  • Receivables and payment status
  • Contract files and images you import or scan

3. Device Permissions

  • Camera: used only to scan contract documents.
  • Photo Library: used only to select property or contract images.
  • Notifications: used only for local rent reminders.

4. Storage and Sync

Core app data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud profile sync, your profile display name and profile photo may sync through your iCloud account settings.

5. Tracking and Data Sharing

RentDue does not use cross-app tracking and does not sell personal data. We do not share your data with third-party advertisers.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete records directly within the app. You can remove locally stored data by deleting the app from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

RentDue is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to: support@rentdue.app
