RentDue Support

Technical Support

This page provides support information for the RentDue iOS app (project name: RentCollect).

Contact

For help, account questions, or bug reports, contact: support@rentdue.app

Typical response time: 1-3 business days.

Common Help Topics

  • How to add or edit properties, units, and leases
  • Managing receivables and marking rent as paid
  • Scanning and attaching lease contract files
  • Notification reminder setup and troubleshooting
  • Privacy, local data storage, and app permissions

Troubleshooting

  • Update to the latest iOS version supported by the App Store release.
  • Force close and reopen the app if a view appears stuck.
  • Confirm Camera/Photo permissions are enabled in iOS Settings if uploads fail.
  • Verify notifications are allowed in iOS Settings if reminders do not appear.

FAQ

Where is my data stored?
Core app records are stored on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.
Do you track me across apps or websites?
No. RentDue does not use cross-app tracking.
Can I delete my data?
Yes. You can delete records in the app, or remove the app from your device.
